Overview

Escape to the magical landscapes of Kashmir with our Lovers' Adventure Package, specially designed for couples. Begin your journey with a romantic Shikara ride on Dal Lake and explore the lush Mughal Gardens in Srinagar. Marvel at the golden meadows of Sonamarg and enjoy the peaceful charm of Pahalgam’s picturesque valleys.

With comfortable stays, seamless transfers, and visits to the most enchanting spots, this tour offers the perfect mix of romance, relaxation, and adventure. Whether you're honeymooning or celebrating love, create memories to treasure forever.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Local Sightseeing


  • Arrival at Srinagar Airport, greeted with a warm welcome.
  • Transfer to your hotel or houseboat for check-in.
  • Explore the iconic Dal Lake with a romantic Shikara ride.
  • Visit the enchanting Mughal Gardens—Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.
  • Take a stroll through the charming old city of Srinagar and admire the Jamia Masjid and Shankaracharya Temple.
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar.


Day 2: A Day Trip to Sonamarg


  • After breakfast, drive to the breathtaking Sonamarg, also known as the "Meadow of Gold."
  • Enjoy picturesque views of snow-clad mountains and lush valleys.
  • Indulge in optional activities like pony rides to Thajiwas Glacier or explore the Sindh River.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening for an overnight stay.


Day 3: Visit Pahalgam


  • Post-breakfast, head to the serene town of Pahalgam, also called the "Valley of Shepherds."
  • En route, visit the Saffron Fields and Awantipora Ruins.
  • Stroll along the Lidder River or opt for pony rides to Baisaran Valley, known as Mini Switzerland.
  • Capture breathtaking views and moments in this romantic setting.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening for an overnight stay.


Day 4: Departure from Srinagar


  • After breakfast, check out and transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.
  • Take back unforgettable memories of your Lovers' Adventure.
Inclusions
  • Pick and Drop Services: Hassle-free transfers to and from Srinagar Airport.
  • 1 Meal Per Day: Enjoy a delicious meal each day during your stay.
  • City Tours: Visit 7 iconic destinations in Srinagar and nearby locations as part of a guided group tour.


Designed for a seamless and memorable experience, these inclusions ensure a worry-free romantic getaway for couples.

Exclusion
  • Additional Services: Expenses for personal requests or optional activities not included in the package.
  • Insurance: Travel, health, or personal insurance is not provided.
  • Food & Drinks: Any meals or beverages beyond the one meal per day included in the package.
  • Tickets: Entry fees to attractions, monuments, or activities are not covered.


Please review the exclusions to plan accordingly and ensure a smooth experience.
