Overview

Discover the enchanting beauty of Kashmir this April, as the valley comes alive with blooming flowers and lush greenery. Experience the serene Dal Lake, the vibrant Mughal Gardens, and the picturesque meadows of Sonamarg and Gulmarg. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike, this tour offers a blend of relaxation and excitement amidst Kashmirs breathtaking springtime landscapes.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Srinagar Sightseeing

  • Pick up from Srinagar Airport.
  • Check-in at your hotel or houseboat.
  • Explore the iconic Dal Lake with a romantic Shikara ride.
  • Visit the stunning Mughal Gardens: Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.
  • Stroll through the old city and visit landmarks like the Shankaracharya Temple.
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar.


Day 2: Sonamarg Excursion

  • After breakfast, depart for Sonamarg, the "Meadow of Gold."
  • Enjoy the breathtaking views of snow-clad mountains and lush green meadows.
  • Optional: Take a pony ride to the Thajiwas Glacier or explore the Sindh River valley.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar.


Day 3: Gulmarg Adventure

  • Early morning departure to Gulmarg, known as the "Meadow of Flowers."
  • Enjoy a thrilling Gondola ride (cable car) to experience panoramic views of the Himalayas.
  • Explore Gulmargs stunning landscapes or opt for seasonal activities like skiing or snowboarding.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar.


Day 4: Pahalgam Excursion

  • Depart for Pahalgam, the "Valley of Shepherds," after breakfast.
  • En route, visit the Saffron Fields and Awantipora Ruins.
  • Explore the scenic Lidder River or opt for a pony ride to Baisaran Valley (Mini Switzerland).
  • Spend the day surrounded by lush greenery and serene beauty.
  • Return to Srinagar in the evening.
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar.


Day 5: Shopping & Departure

  • After breakfast, enjoy some shopping in Srinagars local markets for authentic Kashmiri products like pashmina shawls, saffron, and handicrafts.
  • Transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey.
  • End your tour with fond memories of Kashmirs beauty.
Inclusions
  • Pick and Drop Services: Hassle-free transfers to and from Srinagar Airport.
  • 1 Meal Per Day: Enjoy a delicious meal each day during your stay.
  • City Tours: Visit 7 iconic destinations in Srinagar and nearby locations as part of a guided group tour.

Designed for a seamless and memorable experience, these inclusions ensure a worry-free romantic getaway for couples.

  • Food & Drinks: Any meals or beverages beyond the one meal per day included in the package.
